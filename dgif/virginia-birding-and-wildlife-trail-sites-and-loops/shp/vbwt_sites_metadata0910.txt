  Description:
    Abstract: This dataset contains locational information for Virginia Department of Game and Inland Fisheries' (VDGIF) designated Birding & Wildlife Trail sites located along the Virginia Birding and Wildlife Trail. The coverage includes all of Virginia. These data were compiled by the VDGIF Geographic Information Systems section from the VDGIF's Watchable Wildlife Section. These data are presented as point locations within an ESRI shapefile.
    Purpose: This dataset shows Virginia Department of Game and Inland Fisheries Birding & Wildlife Trail sites located on the trail.
